Dashboard 3: Regional Analysis: Insights by State

Purpose

This dashboard provides a detailed regional analysis by state, focusing on Total Revenue, Average Order Value (AOV), and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth. It empowers users to uncover geographical trends, identify top-performing states, and gain deeper insights into emerging markets.

Key Insights

Regional Trends

  • Total Revenue: The southeast region continues to dominate revenue share, contributing 73.67% of total revenue. São Paulo leads with 37.36% share in total revenue.
  • YoY Growth: São Paulo showed the highest growth rate at 35.29% between 2017 and 2018. Surprisingly, Piauí (PI) followed closely at 35.24% with a revenue increase from $44,615.70 to $60,340.26 ($104,955.96 or 0.71% of total revenue across all years).
  • Emerging Markets: Piauí's growth suggests potential opportunities for expansion in previously underperforming states.
  • Southeast Region: The top 5 states (all from this region) most of the revenue share while growing by an average of 23.18%.

Chart Updates

  • Yearly Columns: Added columns for each year (2017 and 2018) to visualize revenue trends and growth rates within the top 5 states.
  • Sorting Challenge: Sorting by both the selected metric and the year axis remains unresolved but still provides a clear visual for performance trends.

Dashboard Features and Sections

Filters and Selections

Time Slicer: Filters data by year, quarter, or month.
Selected Metric Toggle: Allows toggling between Total Revenue, AOV, and YoY Growth.
YoY Growth KPI Card: Highlights the top states' Year-over-Year growth percentages.
